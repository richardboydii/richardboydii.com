What I Can Do

At this point in my career my strengths are leading and architecting. That takes many forms, from building coalitions to develop new systems to mentoring fellow engineers on new technologies. I love to write code and my current lingua franca is Python, though in a past life I loved Ruby and hope to get better aquainted with Rust and Go in the future. As far as cloud providers go my experience with AWS is deep and I've had the good fortune to be able to alpha and beta test new products in their portfolio. When it comes to automation, I feel that my core purpose is to automate myself out of a job. So what does all that mean? If you need someone to come in and help create momentum for implementing change, help rebuild your infrastructure, or set up a new CI / CD pipeline, I'm your guy.
